About Us

Webber and the Mathematics Club bring students together in a collaborative space to explore new ideas and deepen their understanding of mathematics. The club encourages discussion, problem-solving, and the sharing of different perspectives on various mathematical topics.

Through Webber, students also gain access to fresh insights, engaging articles, and updates on mathematics-related events. Together, they create a dynamic environment where curiosity is encouraged and learning extends beyond the classroom.
